Kavita Vikas Shah


Kavita vikas shah born on 19th Febuary 1978 was interested in cooking by the age of 6. She would help her mother in the kitchen in mixing vegetables and making rotis.At the age of 11 Kavita took her first cooking class at Sanjeev Kapoors Cooking Kitchen and from there on she knew this is what she wanted to do.By the end of college Kavita was selling her inventions in the culinary world to close friends and family.
Later at the age of 24 she got her first bulk order for a birthday party held at the J.W Marriot in Mumbai.At this point she was not that equipped for that huge of an order and she was not able to serve to her fullest.She realised she needed help and she hired Masan and Raju for extra help at such parties.Because of her poor service at her last party she was not able to get another order for the next 3 months but then a business man having a company meetup gave her a chance to serve and with the help on masan and Raju this party was a success.
