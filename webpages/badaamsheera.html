Badaam Sheera

Ingredients:

1/2 cup blanched almonds (badam) , refer handy tip
9 tbsp milk
3 tbsp ghee
4 tbsp sugar
1/4 tsp cardamom (elaichi) powder
a few saffron (kesar) strands

Method:

1. Combine the saffron strands and 1 tbsp of milk in a bowl and mix well. Keep aside.
2. Combine 5 tbsp of milk and 2 tbsp of water in a bowl, mix well and keep aside.
3. Combine the almonds and the remaining 3 tbsp of milk in a mixer and blend into a smooth paste. Keep aside.
4. Heat the ghee in a broad non-stick pan, add the almond paste and cook on a medium flame for 4 minutes, while stirring continuously.
5. Add the milk-water mixture and cook on a medium flame for another 2 minutes, while stirring continuously.
6. Add the sugar, cardamom powder and saffron-milk mixture, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 2 more minutes or till the mixture turns golden brown in colour, while stirring continuously.Serve warm garnished with almond slivers.
