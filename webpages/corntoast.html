Jain Panini Sandwich

Ingredients:

4 toasted whole wheat bread slices , cut into halves
1/2 cup boiled sweet corn kernels (makai ke dane)
2 tbsp grated processed cheese
1 tbsp butter
1/4 cup finely chopped onions
2 tsp plain flour (maida)
3/4 cup milk
2 tbsp finely chopped capsicum
1/2 tsp dried mixed herbs
1/4 tsp mustard (rai / sarson) powder
Salt to add taste

Method:

1. Heat the butter in a broad non-stick pan, add the onions and sauté on a medium flame for 1 to 2 minutes.
2. Add the plain flour and cook on a medium flame for few seconds.
3. Add the milk and cook on a medium flame for 1 to 2 minutes or till the sauce thickens, while stirring continuously.
4. Add the capsicum and mixed herbs, cheese and mix well and cook on a medium flame for 30 seconds.
5. Add the mustard powder, corn and salt and cook on a medium flame for 1 to 2 minutes, while stirring continuously.
6. Divide the corn and cheese topping into 8 equal portions.
7. Spread a portion of the topping evenly on each toasted bread halve.
8. Serve immediately topped with tomato ketchup.
