Jain Burger

Ingredients:

1 Raw Bannana Patty
1 Burger bun
1 tbsp butter
2 tbsp ketchup
1 lettuce leaf
1 large tamato cut into round slices
1 Cheese slice


Method:

1. Cut bun horizontally inot two halves. Heat butter in a flat pan and shallow-fry bun halves until they turn light brown.
2. Heat a non stick pan tp shallow fry the patty and when the pan is hot enough drizzle 2-3 tbsp oil over it. Put the patty on the pan and fry till the surfaces turn golden.
3. Take bottom base bun and spread tamato ketchup over it.
4. Place lettuce leaf followed by the patty.
5. Place the tamato over the patty and spread mayonnaise over that.
6. Then place the cheese on top and cover the burger with the top part of the bun.
